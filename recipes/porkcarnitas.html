Pork Carnitas

Description

Delicious Mexican carnitas made from pork shoulder, braised with lime and different spices for an extraordinary flavor. The pork cooked so tender that it falls apart, served with hot, fresh tortillas and salsa.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 pounds pork shoulder, cut into several big pieces
  • 3 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 crushed clove garlic
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Four 14.5 cans chicken broth

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large Dutch oven over high heat. Season pork shoulder pieces with salt, then arrange in the oven. Cook until browned on every side, about 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in onion, garlic, lime juice, chili power, oregano, and cumin. Pour in chicken broth; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until pork is quite tender, about 2 1/2 hours
  4. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Â°F.
  5. Transfer pork shoulder to a large baking sheet; reserve cooking liquid. Drizzle a small amount of cooking liquid over pieces of pork and lightly powder with salt.
  6. Bake pork in the preheated oven until browned, about 30 minutes. Drizzle more cooking liquid every 10 minutes; use two forks to shred pork while it browns.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
